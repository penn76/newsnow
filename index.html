2005 Toyota Prius reaches 290,000 miles, 10,000 away from 300,000

A Central Ohio family recently bought a 2005 Toyota Prius from their uncle. Whem they took it for a test drive, they were shocked to see it had many miles on it, about 270,000. The uncle used to drive it 20 miles to and from work each day, but it was no surprise to see that it had this much mileage. After using it for a "drive around town" type of car for a while aswell as a 350 mile road trip from Knoxville, it is very well possible that this car can reach 300,000 miles.
